# 02 - What Is a Trend?

## Goal

Learn how to recognize market direction.

---

A trend is simply the **overall direction** of the market.

There are only three possibilities.

### Uptrend

```text
       ▲
     ▲
   ▲
 ▲
```

Price generally keeps making higher prices.

Buyers are stronger.

---

### Downtrend

```text
▲
  ▼
    ▼
      ▼
```

Price generally keeps making lower prices.

Sellers are stronger.

---

### Sideways Market

```text
──────────────
```

Price is moving left and right without a clear direction.

Neither buyers nor sellers are in control.

---

## Important

Price never moves in a straight line.

Even during an uptrend, price will move like this.

```text
▲

▼

▲

▼

▲

▼

▲
```

This is completely normal.
