# 01 - How Markets Move

## Goal

Understand why prices go up and down.

---

## Imagine a Fruit Market

There are only two people.

* A buyer
* A seller

The seller wants **$10** for an apple.

The buyer says:

> "I'll pay $9."

No trade happens.

Another buyer arrives.

He says:

> "I'll pay $10."

Now the trade happens.

The last traded price becomes **$10**.

---

A few minutes later many buyers want apples.

One buyer offers **$11**.

Another offers **$12**.

Now the price becomes **$12**.

Why?

Because buyers are willing to pay more.

---

Now imagine nobody wants apples.

The seller lowers the price.

$12 → $11 → $10 → $9

Price falls because sellers accept lower prices.

---

## Markets Work Exactly the Same Way

Whether it is:

* Gold
* Bitcoin
* EUR/USD
* Apple Stock

Price only moves because buyers and sellers agree on a new price.

---

## Key Idea

Price does **not** move because of indicators.

Indicators only describe what price has already done.

**Price comes first. Indicators come second.**
