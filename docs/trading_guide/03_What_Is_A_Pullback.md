# 03 - What Is a Pullback?

## Goal

Understand why price temporarily moves against the trend.

---

Imagine you are climbing a mountain.

Sometimes you stop to rest.

Then you continue climbing.

The market behaves the same way.

---

Example:

```text
▲

▲

▲

▼

▼

▲

▲

▲
```

The small move downward is called a **pullback**.

The overall trend is still upward.

---

A pullback is **not** necessarily a trend reversal.

It is simply a temporary move in the opposite direction.

---

## Why Traders Like Pullbacks

Imagine buying here.

```text
▲
Buy ❌
```

You are buying after a large move.

Now imagine waiting.

```text
▲

▲

▼

▼

Buy ✅

▲

▲
```

You buy at a better price.

---

## Important

Not every pullback becomes another upward move.

Sometimes a pullback becomes a complete trend reversal.

That is why traders use additional tools like EMA for confirmation.
